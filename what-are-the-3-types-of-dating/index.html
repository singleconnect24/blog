The 3 Types Of Dating

Dating is a complex and diverse phenomenon that can take many forms and have various goals. In this article, we will explore the three main types of dating: Duty Dating, Real Dating, and Courtship.

Duty Dating

Duty Dating is when you are practicing your dating skills and learning how to interact with others in a romantic setting. This type of dating is not based on chemistry or attraction, but rather on the idea of improving your dating skills and experiences. This type of dating is common among people who are new to the dating scene, or who are looking to brush up on their skills after a long absence.

Real Dating

Real Dating is when you are mutually attracted to someone and go out on dates. This type of dating can range from casual and fun to serious and committed. When two people are attracted to each other, they may engage in real dating with the intention of eventually forming a relationship. Real dating often leads to Courtship.

Courtship

Courtship is when both parties are looking for a committed relationship. This type of dating is characterized by an emphasis on building a strong connection and establishing trust. Both parties in a courtship typically have a clear idea of what they want in a relationship and are open to the possibility of eventually marrying.

In addition to these three types of dating, there are also several sub-types that are worth mentioning. These include Mono-partner dating, Poly-partner dating, Omnisexual dating, Monogamous dating, Polyamorous dating, and Open relationship dating.

Mono-partner dating refers to having a single romantic partner, while poly-partner dating involves having multiple partners. Omnisexual dating refers to being attracted to all genders and sexual orientations. Monogamous dating is characterized by having a committed relationship with only one partner, while polyamorous dating involves having multiple romantic relationships with the knowledge and consent of all partners. Open relationship dating refers to a relationship in which both parties are free to pursue other romantic or sexual partners.

In conclusion, the three main types of dating are Duty Dating, Real Dating, and Courtship. Each of these types of dating serves a different purpose and can help individuals achieve their dating and relationship goals. Whether you are looking to improve your dating skills, find a romantic partner, or build a committed relationship, understanding these three types of dating can be a valuable tool in your dating journey.
