Should You Use Your Real Name on Dating Sites?


Using your real name on dating sites can be risky and could lead to your personal information being shared or stolen. Here are some tips to help you stay safe and protect your identity when using dating sites.

1. Don't Use Your Real Name

When creating a username or profile on a dating site, make sure you do not use any aspect of your real name, or any other personally identifiable information such as birthdates - even birth years.

2. Choose a Unique Username

Your username can be searched, and anything tied to that username can come up easily. Choose a unique username that does not include any personal information.

3. Avoid Repetitions

When creating a username, avoid using the same letter or number more than once. This makes it easier for someone to guess your username and gain access to your personal information.

4. Avoid Personal Pronouns

Using personal pronouns such as "I" or "me" in your username can make it easier for someone to guess your identity.

5. Avoid Words Parasites

Words parasites are words that are commonly used in usernames such as "the," "a," and "an." Avoid using these words in your username to make it more difficult for someone to guess your identity.

Using your real name on dating sites can be risky and could lead to your personal information being shared or stolen. By following these tips, you can stay safe and protect your identity when using dating sites.
