How do you know if you're dating someone?

Determining the status of a relationship can often be a confusing and ambiguous process. However, there are some key indicators that can help shed light on the matter.

You Talk About the Future Are you both planning ahead to set up times to hang out, inviting each other to events that are months away, or even discussing going on a trip together? When you are actually dating someone, conversations and plans for the future will come naturally.

Compatibility Test

• Do your interests, values, and goals align with each other?

• Are you both happy spending time together, regardless of what you're doing?

• Do you both support each other's passions and aspirations?

Dating Criteria

• Do you exclusively see each other and not date others?

• Are you both committed to making the relationship work?

• Do you trust each other and feel comfortable discussing sensitive topics?

Romantic Connection

• Do you both have feelings of affection and attraction towards each other?

• Are you both comfortable displaying physical affection towards each other in public?

• Do you both prioritize the relationship and make time for each other?

Emotional Connection

• Do you both feel comfortable opening up and being vulnerable with each other?

• Are you both able to provide emotional support and understanding towards each other?

• Do you both share a deep level of understanding and connection with each other?

In conclusion, when you're dating someone, you should be able to check off most of these criteria. Relationships are unique and may look different for different people, but having a compatibility test, dating criteria, romantic connection, and emotional connection are all key indicators that you are indeed dating someone.
