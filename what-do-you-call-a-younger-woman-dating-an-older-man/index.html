What do you call a younger woman dating an older man? 

In relationships, there is often a term used to describe an older man dating a younger woman. This term is known as a "Manther."

Definition of Manther:

A Manther is a term used to describe an older man who is dating or pursuing a younger woman. It is a play on the word "panther" and is meant to be a playful and lighthearted term.

Usage of Manther:

Manther is mostly used in casual and informal settings, often in jest or humor. However, it can also be used to call out sexist double standards about dating age differences and older men who are "creeping" on younger girls.

In conclusion, the term "Manther" is used to describe an older man dating a younger woman.
