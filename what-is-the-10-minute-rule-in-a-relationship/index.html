What Does the 10 Minute Rule Involve?

The 10 Minute Rule involves investing just ten minutes a day to focus on each other and do simple exercises. These exercises can range from talking about something positive that happened during the day to doing something special for your partner. The idea is to make sure that couples are taking the time to connect with each other and show appreciation for one another.
