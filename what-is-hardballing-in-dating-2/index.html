What is Hardballing in Dating?



Hardballing is a new dating term that describes someone who is clear about their expectations in a relationship. Coined by Logan Ury, director of relationship science at Hinge, hardballing is a product of intentional communication and the ability to be honest and upfront about what you want from a relationship.

Understanding Hardballing

Hardballing is a dating term that refers to the practice of being honest and upfront about what you want from a relationship. It is a way of communicating your expectations to your partner in a direct and clear manner. This can include anything from wanting a serious long-term partnership to a casual fling. Hardballing is about being honest and open about your expectations and not leaving room for misinterpretation.

Benefits of Hardballing

Hardballing has several benefits for both partners in a relationship. By being honest and upfront about your expectations, you can avoid misunderstandings and potential hurt feelings. It also allows both partners to make an informed decision about whether they are willing to meet the other person’s expectations. This can help to ensure that both partners are on the same page and that the relationship is heading in the right direction.

How to Practice Hardballing

If you want to practice hardballing in your relationship, it is important to be honest and open about your expectations. This means being clear about what you want from the relationship and communicating it to your partner. It is also important to be willing to listen to your partner’s expectations and be open to compromise. Finally, it is important to be willing to have difficult conversations and be honest about your feelings.
