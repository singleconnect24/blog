What Does Ashley Madison Cost? A Comprehensive Guide


Ashley Madison is an online dating platform that caters to individuals who are seeking to engage in extramarital affairs. It is one of the most popular dating sites in the world, and its membership fees vary depending on the plan chosen. In this article, we will discuss the cost of Ashley Madison and what you can expect from each plan.

Basic Plan

The Basic plan is the most affordable option on Ashley Madison. It costs $59 and includes 100 credits. Credits are used to send messages and gifts, and can be purchased in bundles of 100, 500, and 1000.

Classic Plan

The Classic plan costs $169 and includes 500 credits. This plan is ideal for those who are looking to engage in more conversations and gifts on the platform.

Elite Plan

The Elite plan is the most expensive option on Ashley Madison. It costs $289 and includes 1000 credits. This plan is ideal for those who are looking to engage in a lot of conversations and gifts on the platform.
