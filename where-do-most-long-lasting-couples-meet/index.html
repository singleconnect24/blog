Where do most long lasting couples meet?

When it comes to finding a long-lasting relationship, the answer to the question “Where do most long lasting couples meet?” is not always straightforward. Although the traditional route of meeting through family or friends is still the most common way for couples to meet, the internet has opened up a whole new world of possibilities for couples to find each other.

Introduction through Family or Friends

Introductions through family or friends are the most common way people meet a partner, with many couples citing this as the way they met their significant other. This is often the case for couples who have been together for a long time, as they may have been introduced to each other through a mutual friend or family member. This is also the case for couples who are just starting out, as they may have been set up by a family member or friend.

Meeting Partners Online

Although meeting through family or friends is still the most common way for couples to meet, meeting partners online is becoming increasingly popular among some groups. With the rise of online dating apps and websites, more and more people are turning to the internet to find a partner. This is especially true for those who are not in the same social circles as their potential partners, as they may not have the same opportunities to meet someone through family or friends.

Looking at adults whose dating days are behind them – at least for the time being – friends and family were the most common source in helping them find a match. According to a survey conducted by the Pew Research Center in August 2020, about a quarter of adults aged 50 and older who are married or in a committed relationship met their partner through a friend or family member.
