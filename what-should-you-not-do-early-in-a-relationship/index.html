What should you not do early in a relationship?

Starting a new relationship can be an exciting time, but it's important to avoid common mistakes that can jeopardize the foundation of the relationship. Here are some things to avoid doing early in a relationship, according to experts.

Coming on Too Strong:

One of the most common mistakes in the early stages of a relationship is coming on too strong. This can include constantly texting, calling, or expressing intense emotions too soon. This type of behavior can be overwhelming and push your partner away.

Being Too Rigid in What You Want:

Another mistake to avoid is being too rigid in what you want. This can include having a strict list of what you're looking for in a partner or having a set idea of how the relationship should progress. Being too rigid can limit the potential for growth and flexibility in the relationship.

Performing for Them:

It's also important to avoid performing for your partner. This can include trying too hard to impress them, putting on a show, or trying to be someone you're not. Being authentic is key in building a strong and healthy relationship.

Not Learning from Past Relationships:

Another mistake to avoid is not learning from past relationships. This can include repeating patterns that have caused problems in the past or ignoring red flags. It's important to reflect on past relationships and use those experiences to grow and build a healthy relationship.

Moving Too Fast:

Moving too fast is another common mistake in the early stages of a relationship. This can include rushing into a physical relationship, making major life decisions too soon, or moving in together before you're ready.

Ignoring Red Flags:

It's also important to avoid ignoring red flags early in a relationship. This can include ignoring warning signs or disregarding behavior that is not in line with your values or needs.

Posting Too Much Online:

Posting too much about your relationship online is another mistake to avoid. This can include oversharing personal details or posting too many pictures or updates. It's important to maintain privacy and respect boundaries in a relationship.

Not Establishing Clear Boundaries Early On:

Finally, it's important to avoid not establishing clear boundaries early on in a relationship. This can include not setting limits on physical or emotional intimacy, not communicating your needs and wants, or not respecting your partner's boundaries.

In conclusion, avoiding common mistakes in the early stages of a relationship can help lay the foundation for a strong and healthy relationship. These mistakes include coming on too strong, being too rigid in what you want, performing for your partner, not learning from past relationships, moving too fast, ignoring red flags, posting too much online, and not establishing clear boundaries. By avoiding these mistakes, you can build a strong and healthy relationship from the start.
