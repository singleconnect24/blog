How often do 60 year olds make?

As people age, their sexual habits and desires can change. Understanding these changes can help individuals and couples make informed decisions about their sexual health and well-being.

Average Frequency of Sexual Activity

According to a recent survey, the average person aged 50 to 59 reported having sex 38 times per year, while people in their 60s reported having sex an average of 25 times per year.

Factors Affecting Sexual Frequency

There are a number of factors that can impact sexual frequency in older adults, including physical health, relationship status, and personal preferences. Chronic illnesses and disabilities can make sexual activity more difficult, while good physical health can contribute to a more active sex life.

Importance of Communication

Regardless of frequency, it's important for individuals and couples to have open and honest conversations about their sexual needs and desires. This can help ensure that both partners feel satisfied and fulfilled in their relationship.
