7 Red Flags to Look Out for on a First Date


Going on a first date can be exciting, but it can also be nerve-wracking. To make sure you have a positive experience, it’s important to be aware of potential red flags. Here are seven red flags to look out for on a first date.

1. Too Much Touching

If your date is overly touchy or sexual right away, this could be a red flag. If you’ve made your physical and sexual boundaries clear in the beginning, it’s important to stick to them.

2. Complaining and Criticism

If your date is constantly complaining and overly critical, especially of you, this could be a sign that they’re not the right person for you.

3. Showing Up Late

If your date shows up late without informing you ahead of time, this could be a sign that they’re not taking the date seriously.

4. No Interest in Conversation

If your date is not interested in getting to know you or having a conversation, this could be a sign that they’re not interested in you.

5. Excessive Drinking

If your date is drinking excessively, this could be a sign that they’re trying to mask their true feelings or that they’re not taking the date seriously.

6. Unwillingness to Pay

If your date is unwilling to pay for the date or split the bill, this could be a sign that they’re not interested in you or the date.

7. Controlling Behavior

If your date is overly controlling or trying to dictate how you should act or dress, this could be a sign that they’re not the right person for you.

These are just a few of the red flags to look out for on a first date. It’s important to trust your instincts and to be aware of any potential red flags. If you notice any of these red flags, it’s best to end the date and move on.
