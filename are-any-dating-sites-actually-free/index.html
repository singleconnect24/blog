What Is OkCupid?
OkCupid is one of the most popular dating sites on the web. It was launched in 2004 and has been completely free ever since. The site allows users to create a profile, search for compatible dates, receive potential matches, flirt, send and receive messages, and more.

Is OkCupid Truly Free?
Yes, OkCupid is completely free. The team behind the site has promised that it will remain free as long as the site is running. This means that users don't have to pay to create a profile, search for compatible dates, receive a potential match, flirt, send and receive messages, or anything else.

• Hinge: This app is free to use and allows users to match with potential dates based on their shared interests.
