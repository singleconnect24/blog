The Cost of Using Plenty of Fish (POF)


Plenty of Fish (POF) is one of the most popular online dating sites, and it offers a range of subscription plans for users. But how much does POF cost a month?

Subscription Plans

POF offers a range of subscription plans for users, and the cost of using the site depends on the plan chosen. The plans available are:

• Single Month Plan: This plan costs $19.99 per month.
• Three Month Plan: This plan costs $12.99 per month, billed as a single payment of $38.97.
• Six Month Plan: This plan costs $9.99 per month, billed as a single payment of $59.94.
• Eight Month Plan: This plan costs $9.99 per month, billed as a single payment of $79.92.

Additional Features

In addition to the subscription plans, POF also offers additional features that users can purchase. These include:

• Profile Boost: This feature boosts your profile in the search results, making it more visible to other users. It costs $6.78 per boost.
• Tokens: Tokens are used to purchase virtual gifts for other users. They cost $1.00 each.
• Super Yes: This feature allows you to send a Super Yes to someone, which increases your chances of getting a match. It costs $1.99 per Super Yes.

Overall, the cost of using POF depends on the subscription plan and additional features chosen. Plans start at $19.99 for a single month, or as low as $9.99 per month for an eight-month commitment. Additional features such as Profile Boosts, Tokens, and Super Yes can also be purchased for an additional cost.
