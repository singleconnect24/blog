What is the number 1 dating app in USA?



Tinder is the number one dating app in the United States, according to the latest figures from June 2022. The app was downloaded an impressive 957 thousand times in the month, making it the most popular dating app in the country.

Bumble, the female-first dating app, came in second place with 786 thousand downloads. Hinge, which is focused on creating long-term connections, was the third most downloaded app with 545 thousand downloads.

Tinder is a location-based dating app that uses a simple swipe system to match users. It has been around since 2012 and is available in 190 countries. It is one of the most popular dating apps in the world, with an estimated 50 million users.

Bumble was founded in 2014 and is a female-first dating app. It requires women to make the first move and has been credited with helping to reduce harassment and inappropriate behavior on dating apps. It is available in 150 countries and has over 80 million users.

Hinge is a dating app that is focused on creating long-term connections. It was founded in 2012 and is available in over 20 countries. It is designed to help users find more meaningful relationships by using an algorithm that takes into account interests, values, and personality traits.

These three apps are the most popular dating apps in the United States, but there are many other apps available. These include Grindr, OkCupid, and Plenty of Fish. All of these apps have their own unique features and are designed to help users find the perfect match.
