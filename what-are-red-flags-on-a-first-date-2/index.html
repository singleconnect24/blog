What are red flags on a first date?


When it comes to dating, it’s important to be aware of potential red flags that could signal a bad relationship. Red flags on a first date can be anything from a lack of communication to a date who is overly critical or too touchy. Knowing what to look out for can help you avoid a bad relationship before it starts.

What Are the Most Common Red Flags on a First Date?

When it comes to red flags on a first date, the most common ones are a lack of communication, a date who is overly critical, and someone who is too touchy.

A Lack of Communication

One of the most common red flags on a first date is a lack of communication. If your date is not engaging in conversation or is not responding to your questions, it could be a sign that they are not interested in getting to know you.

Someone Who Is Overly Critical

Another red flag is a date who is overly critical. If your date is constantly complaining or criticizing you or the things you say, it could be a sign that they are not interested in a relationship.

Someone Who Is Too Touchy

Another red flag is a date who is too touchy. If your date is being overly physical or sexual right away, even if you have made your physical and sexual boundaries clear, it could be a sign that they are not interested in a relationship.

Other Red Flags

Other red flags include a date who shows up late, doesn’t inform you ahead of time, or is overly negative. It’s important to be aware of these red flags and to trust your instincts if something doesn’t feel right.
