How do you tell if a guy likes you?

Determining whether a guy likes you or not can be challenging. The signals may be subtle, and it is important to know what to look for. This article provides 22 subtle signs from dating experts that can indicate that a guy is interested in you.

He Leans Toward You During Conversation

If a guy leans towards you while talking, it may indicate that he is interested in you and wants to be closer to you.

Angles His Body Toward You in the Room

If a guy positions himself towards you when in a room, it can be a sign that he wants to be near you and is paying attention to you.

Finds Small Ways to Compliment You

If a guy compliments you regularly, it can be a sign that he is interested in you and wants to make a positive impression.

Makes Eye Contact

Eye contact is a powerful tool in communication, and if a guy makes eye contact with you frequently, it may be a sign that he likes you.

Steals a Glance at You

If a guy steals a quick look at you when you're not looking, it may indicate that he is interested in you and wants to catch a glimpse of you.

Singles You Out in a Group

If a guy singles you out in a group, it can be a sign that he wants to have your attention and is interested in getting to know you better.

Seems Drawn to You in the Room

If a guy seems drawn to you and wants to be near you in a room, it can be a sign that he likes you and wants to be close to you.

These are just a few of the many subtle signs that a guy may be interested in you. It's important to pay attention to these signals and to be confident in yourself. If a guy likes you, he will make it known, and you should trust your instincts when it comes to determining if he is truly interested.
