What are the 3 P's of dating? 

Dating can be a complex and challenging process. It's essential to take into account various factors that can make or break a date. One way to approach dating is to use the "three p's" test. This article will explore what the three p's are and how they can help you have a successful date.

Planned Ahead:

The first "p" in the three p's of dating is planning. It's crucial to plan ahead for a date to ensure that everything runs smoothly. This means considering the date's location, time, and activities in advance. A well-planned date can help set the right mood and make the experience more enjoyable for both parties.

Paid For:

The second "p" in the three p's of dating is payment. It's customary for the person who asked for the date to pay for it. This can help establish a level of respect and shows that the person values and appreciates the other person's time and company. It's important to discuss payment arrangements before the date to avoid any awkwardness or misunderstandings.

Paired Off:

The third and final "p" in the three p's of dating is pairing off. This means that the date should be between two individuals who are attracted to each other and want to spend time together. A successful date should involve a mutual connection and understanding between the two people involved.

In conclusion, the three p's of dating are an essential tool for a successful date. By following the three p's, you can ensure that your date is well-planned, paid for, and paired off. These considerations can help create a positive and enjoyable dating experience that can lead to a lasting relationship.
