What is Breadcrumbing in dating?

Breadcrumbing is a term used to describe a type of behavior that has become common in the world of dating. It involves leading someone on with flirty messages, giving them false hope, and leaving them feeling confused and unsure about the status of the relationship.

What is Breadcrumbing?

Breadcrumbing is a form of communication in which a person sends flirty messages, without making any real effort to pursue a relationship. The person sends out "breadcrumbs" of affection, with the hope of keeping the other person interested, without actually making a real commitment. The breadcrumbs can take the form of messages, comments, or likes on social media.

Effects of Breadcrumbing

Breadcrumbing can have a negative impact on the person being breadcrumbed. They may feel confused and unsure about the status of the relationship, leading to feelings of frustration, disappointment, and insecurity. It can also be damaging to one's self-esteem and can cause them to question their worth and attractiveness.

Breadcrumbing is a manipulative form of communication that can be hurtful to those on the receiving end. It's important to be clear and direct in our communication with others, and to not lead them on with false hope. If you're not interested in pursuing a relationship, it's better to be honest and clear with the other person, rather than breadcrumbing them.
