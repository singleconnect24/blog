How Can You Tell If a Guy Is Real Online?


Are you considering getting to know someone online, but you’re not sure if they’re real? It’s a valid concern, especially in today’s digital age. Thankfully, there are some telltale signs that can help you determine if a guy is real online.

What Is Catfishing?

Before we get into the signs of a real guy online, let’s define what catfishing is. Catfishing is when someone creates a false identity online to deceive people. They may use a fake name, age, and photos to create a persona that is not their own. They may also use a false backstory to make their profile more convincing.

Signs of a Real Guy Online

There are a few key signs that can help you determine if a guy is real online.

1. They’re Open About Their Identity: A real guy online will be open about their identity. They won’t hide their name, age, or photos. They will also be willing to provide more information about themselves if asked.

2. They Have a Consistent Story: A real guy online will have a consistent story. They won’t change their story or make up new details. If they do, it’s a sign that they’re not being truthful.

3. They’re Willing to Meet in Person: A real guy online will be willing to meet in person. They won’t make excuses or try to avoid meeting up. If they’re not willing to meet in person, it’s a sign that they’re not who they say they are.

4. They Have Real Friends: A real guy online will have real friends. They won’t have a lot of fake friends or followers. If they do, it’s a sign that they’re not being truthful.

It can be difficult to tell if a guy is real online, but there are some telltale signs that can help you determine if they’re who they say they are. If they’re open about their identity, have a consistent story, are willing to meet in person, and have real friends, then they’re likely to be real.
