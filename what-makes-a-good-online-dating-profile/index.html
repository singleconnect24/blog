What makes a good online dating profile?

Online dating has become increasingly popular in recent years, as more and more people are turning to the internet to find potential partners. But what makes a good online dating profile? Creating an effective profile is essential for successful online dating, as it will help you attract the right kind of people and make sure that your messages get noticed. In this article, we’ll explore eight tips for creating an amazing online dating profile.

What Makes a Good Online Dating Profile?


Creating an effective online dating profile can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. With the right tips and tricks, you can create a profile that stands out from the crowd and gets noticed. Here are eight tips for creating an amazing online dating profile:

1. Aim for between five and seven photos.

When it comes to creating an online dating profile, photos are key. Aim for between five and seven photos that showcase your personality and interests. Include a mix of headshots, full-body shots, and lifestyle shots to give potential matches a better idea of who you are.

2. Bolster your profile with lifestyle shots.

In addition to headshots and full-body shots, include lifestyle shots that show off your hobbies and interests. This will help potential matches get a better sense of who you are and what you’re looking for.

3. Keep the focus on you.

When creating your profile, make sure the focus is on you. Avoid posting photos with other people in them, as this can be confusing for potential matches.

4. Consider using only one selfie, if any at all.

Selfies can be a great way to show off your personality, but they can also be a bit overdone. Consider using only one selfie, if any at all, and focus on other types of photos to showcase your interests and hobbies.

5. Be as pithy as possible.

When writing your profile, keep it concise and to the point. Avoid rambling on about yourself and instead focus on the qualities and interests that make you unique.

6. Include a conversational opening.

Include a conversational opening in your profile to make it easier for potential matches to start a conversation with you. This could be a joke, a question, or a statement about something you’re passionate about.

7. Stay positive while being specific about what you’re looking for.

When writing your profile, stay positive and be specific about what you’re looking for. Avoid negative language and focus on the qualities and interests that make you an ideal match.

Creating an effective online dating profile can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. With the right tips and tricks, you can create a profile that stands out from the crowd and gets noticed. Aim for between five and seven photos, bolster your profile with lifestyle shots, keep the focus on you, consider using only one selfie, if any at all, be as pithy as possible, include a conversational opening, and stay positive while being specific about what you’re looking for. Following these tips will help you create an amazing online dating profile that will attract the right kind of people and make sure that your messages get noticed.
