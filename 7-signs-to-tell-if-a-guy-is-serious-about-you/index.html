7 Signs To Tell if a Guy Is Serious About You


Is your guy serious about you? If you’re wondering whether he’s ready to take the next step, here are seven signs to look out for.

1. He Listens to You

If a guy is serious about you, he will really listen and he won't take anything you say for granted. He will be interested in your thoughts and opinions and will take them into consideration.

2. He Makes Future Plans

If a guy is serious about you, he will be thinking about the future and making plans with you in mind. He will be discussing trips you could take together, activities you could do, and other plans that involve both of you.

3. He Checks Up on You

A guy who is serious about you will want to make sure you are okay. He will call or text you just to check in and see how you are doing.

4. He Helps You with Any Problem

If a guy is serious about you, he will be there to help you with any problem. He will be willing to lend a hand and offer advice and support when you need it.

5. He Makes You a Priority

A guy who is serious about you will make you a priority in his life. He will make time for you and make sure you are always a part of his plans.

6. He Does Thoughtful Things

A guy who is serious about you will do thoughtful things to show you how much he cares. He will surprise you with gifts, take you out to dinner, or do something special just for you.

7. He Introduces You to His Friends

If a guy is serious about you, he will want to introduce you to his friends and family. He will be proud to show you off and will want to share his life with you.

Signs That a Man Is Serious About You

If you’re wondering if a man is serious about you, look out for these seven signs. From listening to you to making you a priority, these are surefire signs that he’s ready to take the next step.
