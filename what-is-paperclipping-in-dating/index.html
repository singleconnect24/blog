What is Paperclipping in Dating?


Paperclipping is a term used to describe a certain type of behavior in dating. It is when an ex-partner reaches out intermittently, not because they are interested in getting back together, but rather to keep the other person as an option. This type of behavior is similar to breadcrumbing and is often used by narcissists.
