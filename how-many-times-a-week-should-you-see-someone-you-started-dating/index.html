How many times a week should you see someone you started dating? 

Dating is a crucial stage in a relationship where two people are getting to know each other better. It is an exciting time, but it can also be a bit overwhelming, especially when it comes to deciding how often you should see each other.

Aiming for a Date a Week:

According to relationship experts, a good benchmark to aim for is one date a week. This frequency of seeing each other is enough to give time to the relationship and keep the connection strong, without meeting up so little that the connection fizzles out.

Avoiding Over-Attachment:

In the early stages of infatuation, it is tempting to want to see someone you are dating as often as possible. However, this can lead to over-attachment and can be detrimental to the relationship in the long run.

Ultimately, the frequency of seeing someone you are dating will depend on your individual schedules, preferences, and the pace at which you both want the relationship to grow. But, a good rule of thumb is to aim for one date a week.
