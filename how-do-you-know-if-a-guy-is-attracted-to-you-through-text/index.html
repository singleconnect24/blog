How do you know if a guy is attracted to you through text?


Texting has become a popular way to communicate with people, and it can be a great way to get to know someone better. But how do you know if a guy is attracted to you through text? There are some tell-tale signs that can help you figure out if he’s interested.

What to Look for When a Guy Texts You

When it comes to deciphering whether a guy is interested in you or not, there are some key signs to look for. Here are some of the most common signs that a guy is attracted to you through text:

He Texts Back Immediately: If a guy is interested in you, he’ll usually respond to your texts right away. He’ll also keep the conversation going, so if you’re texting back and forth for a while, it’s a good sign that he’s into you.

He Wishes You Weren’t Just Texting: If a guy is attracted to you, he’ll often express that he wishes you were together in person. He might say something like, “I wish we could be together right now” or “I wish we could hang out soon.”

He Talks About Things You Both Would Be Doing If He Was There: If a guy is interested in you, he’ll often talk about things you could do together if he was there. He might mention going to a movie, going out to dinner, or just hanging out.

He Lets You Know If He Can’t Text Back Right Now: If a guy is into you, he’ll usually let you know if he can’t text back right away. He might say something like, “I’m busy right now, but I’ll text you later.”

He Often Uses Flirty Emojis: If a guy is attracted to you, he’ll often use flirty emojis when he texts you. He might use a wink face, a heart, or a kissy face.

He Likes Hearing Your Stories: If a guy is interested in you, he’ll usually be interested in hearing about your day and your stories. He’ll ask questions and show genuine interest in what you have to say.

He Wants to Know More About You: If a guy is attracted to you, he’ll usually want to know more about you. He’ll ask questions about your family, your hobbies, and your interests.

He Writes You Long Texts: If a guy is into you, he’ll usually write you longer texts. He’ll take the time to write out his thoughts and feelings, and he’ll often ask you questions.

These are just some of the signs that a guy is attracted to you through text. If you’re looking for more clues, pay attention to how often he texts you, how quickly he responds, and how much effort he puts into the conversation.

Conclusion

If you’re trying to figure out if a guy is attracted to you through text, look for signs like he texts back immediately, he wishes you weren’t just texting, he talks about things you both would be doing if he was there, he lets you know if he can’t text back right now, he often uses flirty emojis, he likes hearing your stories, he wants to know more about you, and he writes you long texts. Pay attention to these signs, and you’ll be able to tell if a guy is interested in you or not.
