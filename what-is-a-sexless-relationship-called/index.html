What is a sexless relationship called?

Couples in a relationship may go through different phases in their intimacy, and a lack of sexual activity is not uncommon. However, there is no widely recognized term for this type of relationship. The following article explores different perspectives on the topic.

The Definition of a Sexless Relationship

Celibacy refers to the act of abstaining from sexual activity, but it doesn't necessarily imply that both partners are in agreement or happy with the situation. A sexless relationship, on the other hand, refers to a relationship where there is little to no sexual intimacy.

Prevalence of Sexless Relationships

Statistics on the prevalence of sexless relationships are limited, as many couples may not publicly acknowledge their lack of sexual activity. However, it's not uncommon for married or cohabiting couples to be in a sexless relationship. It's also important to note that some couples may be happily or resignedly not having sex.

Asexuality and Sexless Relationships

Asexuality, or the lack of sexual attraction, has become a buzzword in recent years. Some individuals identify as asexual and may choose to be in a relationship without sexual intimacy. It's important to respect individual preferences and choices in relationships, whether it's a sexual relationship or a sexless one.

Dating and Online Dating in a Sexless Relationship

Dating and online dating can present challenges for individuals in a sexless relationship. While some may prefer to be in a relationship without sexual intimacy, others may feel frustrated or unfulfilled. It's important for individuals to communicate their needs and preferences with their partner, and to find a partner who shares similar values and perspectives.

The Reality of Sexless Relationships in the USA

The reality of sexless relationships in the USA is that they are more common than many people realize. It's important for individuals in a relationship to discuss their needs and preferences in terms of sexual intimacy, and to work together to find a solution that works for both partners. Whether it's a sexual relationship or a sexless one, what's most important is that both partners are happy and fulfilled.

In conclusion, there is no proper name for a sexless relationship, but it's a common issue that couples may face. It's important to respect individual preferences and choices, and for couples to openly communicate and work together to find a solution that works for both partners.
