How long should a first date last?

Dating can be an exciting and nerve-wracking experience, and the length of a first date can make a big impact. A dating etiquette expert shared his opinion on the ideal first date length. According to Ryan Seacrest, the perfect first date should last 90 minutes.

The Importance of Time:

The amount of time a first date lasts is crucial in determining if there is a spark between the two individuals. If the date goes on for too long, it can lead to boredom and discomfort, and if it's too short, it may not provide enough time to get to know each other.

Advantages of a 90-Minute Date:

A 90-minute date strikes the perfect balance between being long enough to gauge if there is a spark and short enough to leave both individuals wanting more. This time frame provides ample opportunity for conversation, getting to know each other, and discovering common interests.

Creating a Positive Experience:

Keeping the first date to 90 minutes can create a positive experience for both individuals. It helps to establish a good first impression and leaves a lasting impact, increasing the chances of a second date. Furthermore, it avoids the awkwardness of a date that goes on for too long, which can lead to an uncomfortable end to the evening.

In conclusion, Ryan Seacrest's recommendation for a 90-minute first date is a great guideline to follow. It provides enough time to determine if there is a spark while also keeping the date light, enjoyable, and memorable. Remember, the key to a successful first date is to relax, be yourself, and have a good time!
