How Often Should Couples Be Intimate?


When it comes to relationships, one of the most important aspects is physical intimacy. Knowing how often couples should be intimate is essential for a healthy relationship.

Age and Frequency

Studies have shown that couples who are in their 40s and 50s tend to have sex around once a week, while couples in their 20s and 30s tend to have sex around twice a week. This statistic may vary depending on the couple, but it is a good baseline to consider.

Importance of Communication

No matter what age you are, communication is key when it comes to physical intimacy. It is important to talk to your partner about how often you both would like to be intimate. This can help to ensure that both partners are comfortable and satisfied with the frequency.

Benefits of Intimacy

Physical intimacy can bring many benefits to a relationship. It can help to build trust and connection between partners, as well as increasing feelings of love and happiness. Intimacy can also help to reduce stress and anxiety, as well as improving physical health.


When it comes to physical intimacy, there is no one-size-fits-all answer. It is important to talk to your partner and find a frequency that works for both of you.
