How long should you date before deleting dating app?


The dating app world can be a confusing place. It can be hard to know when it’s time to delete your dating app and move on to something more serious. The answer to this question is not a simple one, as it depends on the individual situation.

Take Stock of the Situation After Three to Five Dates

When it comes to dating apps, it’s important to take stock of the situation after three to five dates. Ask yourself if you’re still interested in continuing to date this person, or if you’re ready to move on. If you’re still interested, it’s time to start considering deleting your dating app.

Still Not Ready to Hit the “X”?

If you’re not quite ready to hit the “x” on your dating app, but you don’t want to end it either, it’s important to set some boundaries. Agree to stay off the dating app for a couple of months and mean it. This will give you both the time and space to see if the relationship is something you both want to pursue.

When it comes to deleting your dating app, there is no one-size-fits-all answer. It’s important to take stock of the situation after three to five dates and decide if you’re ready to move on or if you want to continue dating. If you’re not ready to delete your dating app, agree to stay off it for a couple of months and see where the relationship goes.
