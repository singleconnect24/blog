What are red flags on a first date?

A first date is an opportunity to get to know someone new and see if there is potential for a relationship. However, not all first dates are successful, and some people may display warning signs that indicate a lack of compatibility. In this article, we will discuss some common red flags to watch for on a first date.

Too Touchy and Sexual

One red flag is a date who is too touchy and sexual from the start, especially if you have made your physical and sexual boundaries clear from the beginning. This behavior can be a sign of a lack of respect for your boundaries and could indicate that the person is only interested in physical intimacy.

Complaining and Critical Attitude

Another red flag is a date who complains all the time during the date and is overly critical, especially of you. This can be a sign that the person is unhappy with themselves and may have negative attitudes towards others. A critical attitude can also indicate a lack of empathy and the potential for future conflicts.

Late Arrival

A date who shows up late without informing you ahead of time can be a red flag. This could indicate that the person is unreliable or disrespectful of your time. It could also be a sign that they don't take the date or your time seriously.

Red flags on a first date can be a sign that a relationship with the person is not likely to be successful. Pay attention to these warning signs and trust your instincts if something feels off. Remember that a first date is an opportunity to get to know someone, but it's also important to be mindful of your own safety and comfort.
