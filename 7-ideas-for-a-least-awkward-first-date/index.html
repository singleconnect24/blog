7 Ideas for a Least Awkward First Date

Finding the right activity for a first date can be a challenge. If you want to make sure that your date is comfortable and the conversation flows, you should choose an activity-based date. Arlin Cuncic, MA, author of "Therapy in Focus: What to Expect from CBT for Social Anxiety Disorder" and "7 Weeks to Reduce Anxiety," suggests that activity-based dates can help reduce the awkwardness of a first date. Here are seven ideas for a least awkward first date.

Attend an Outdoor Concert

Outdoor concerts are a great way to spend time together while listening to music. This type of date can be a great way to break the ice and get to know each other. Plus, you can enjoy the music and the atmosphere without feeling the pressure of having to talk.

Attend a Comedy Show

Going to a comedy show is a great way to get to know each other and have a good laugh. You can bond over the jokes and the shared experience. Plus, the laughter can help to ease any tension or awkwardness.

Attend a Play

Going to a play is a great way to get to know each other and enjoy a shared experience. You can discuss the play after the show and get to know each other better. Plus, the play can provide a distraction from any awkwardness.

Attend a Poetry Reading

Attending a poetry reading is a great way to get to know each other and enjoy a shared experience. You can discuss the poetry after the reading and get to know each other better. Plus, the poetry can provide a distraction from any awkwardness.

Attend a Sporting Event

Going to a sporting event is a great way to get to know each other and have a good time. You can bond over the game and the shared experience. Plus, the excitement of the game can help to ease any tension or awkwardness.

Go Bowling

Bowling is a great way to spend time together and have a good time. You can bond over the game and the shared experience. Plus, the fun of the game can help to ease any tension or awkwardness.

Go Ice Skating

Ice skating is a great way to spend time together and have a good time. You can bond over the game and the shared experience. Plus, the fun of the game can help to ease any tension or awkwardness.

Finding the right activity for a first date can be a challenge. But if you choose an activity-based date, you can make sure that your date is comfortable and the conversation flows. With these seven ideas for a least awkward first date, you can make sure that your date is a success.
