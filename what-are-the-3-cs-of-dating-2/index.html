What are the 3 C's of dating?

When it comes to building a healthy relationship, communication, compromise, and commitment are essential. These three elements, also known as the 3 C's of dating, are the foundation of any successful relationship, whether it's romantic or not.

In this article, we'll discuss the importance of the 3 C's and how you can use them to improve your dating experiences.

Relationships that are 50/50 are flatlined

Many people believe that a successful relationship should be 50/50, meaning that each partner contributes equally to the relationship. While this idea may seem reasonable in theory, it doesn't always work in practice. Relationships are dynamic and often require more from one partner than the other at different times. The 3 C's help couples navigate these ups and downs.

Relationship dynamics will go up and down based on communication, compromise, and commitment, the 3 C’s.

Communication

Communication is the key to any successful relationship. It's essential to communicate your needs, wants, and feelings openly and honestly. Good communication also involves actively listening to your partner and trying to understand their perspective. When you communicate effectively, you can resolve conflicts, build trust, and strengthen your connection.

Compromise

Compromise is another critical component of a healthy relationship. It's impossible to agree on everything all the time, so it's important to find a middle ground. Compromise means that both partners give a little to reach a mutually beneficial solution. It's essential to remember that compromise is not about giving up what you want, but rather finding a solution that works for both partners.

Commitment

Commitment is the glue that holds relationships together. It means that you are dedicated to your partner and the relationship and are willing to put in the effort to make it work. Commitment involves being there for your partner, supporting them through difficult times, and prioritizing your relationship above other things.

Spring 2019 Speaker Elder Little Brown Bear discusses how to use the 3C’s to build healthy relationships within romantic relationships and non-romantic relationships alike

Elder Little Brown Bear, a speaker from Spring 2019, emphasizes that the 3 C's are not only essential for romantic relationships but also for all types of relationships. Whether it's with friends, family, or coworkers, communication, compromise, and commitment are key to building healthy and fulfilling relationships.

In conclusion, the 3 C's of dating are critical to building healthy and successful relationships. Good communication, compromise, and commitment can help you navigate the ups and downs of any relationship. Remember, relationships are dynamic and require effort from both partners. By using the 3 C's, you can build strong and meaningful connections with others, whether they are romantic or not.
