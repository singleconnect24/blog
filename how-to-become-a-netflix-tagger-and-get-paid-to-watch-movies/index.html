How to Become a Netflix Tagger and Get Paid to Watch Movies


Do you love watching movies and TV shows? Do you want to get paid for doing it? If so, then you should consider becoming a Netflix Tagger. Netflix Taggers are part-time employees who get paid to watch Netflix content and assign tags to it. This helps the streaming service recommend other shows and movies to viewers after they finish watching. Here's how you can become a Netflix Tagger and get paid to watch movies.

What is a Netflix Tagger?

A Netflix Tagger is a part-time employee who is hired to watch Netflix content and assign tags to it. These tags help the streaming service recommend other shows and movies to viewers after they finish watching. Netflix Taggers are paid for their work and can work from home.

What Does a Netflix Tagger Do?

Netflix Taggers watch Netflix content and assign tags to it. They are responsible for tagging the content with relevant keywords and phrases that will help the streaming service recommend other shows and movies to viewers after they finish watching.

What Qualifications Do I Need to Become a Netflix Tagger?

Netflix Taggers need to have an understanding of the streaming service's content and the ability to accurately tag it. They should also have good communication skills and be able to work independently.

How Do I Become a Netflix Tagger?

Netflix does not currently have an official program for hiring Taggers. However, you can apply for positions through third-party websites such as Upwork and Fiverr. You can also keep an eye out for job postings on the Netflix Careers website.

How Much Do Netflix Taggers Get Paid?

The amount that Netflix Taggers get paid varies depending on the job. However, most Taggers make between $10 and $20 per hour.
