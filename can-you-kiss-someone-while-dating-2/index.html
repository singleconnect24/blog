Can you kiss someone while dating?

Introduction

- Kissing is an intimate act that can bring couples closer together

Decision Making

- No two first dates are alike, therefore, the decision to kiss or not is unique to each situation

- It is important to listen to your gut instincts and make a decision that feels comfortable and right for you

Kissing in the Moment

- In most cases, kissing on a first date simply happens in the moment

- If both parties are feeling a connection, the kiss may come naturally without any pressure or expectations

Conclusion
