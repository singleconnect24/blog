How often is too often to see someone when you start dating?

The Ideal Frequency

According to relationship expert, twice a week is the ideal frequency for seeing someone when you start dating. This balance provides enough time to spend together and also allows for personal space and time to reflect on feelings.

The Importance of Space

Having space and time away from your partner is crucial in the early stages of a relationship. It allows for deeper feelings to surface and helps identify any potential issues or problems with your partner. Maintaining a balance of time spent together and apart is key to a healthy relationship.

When starting a new relationship, it's important to strike a balance between spending time together and having personal space. Seeing each other twice a week is a good starting point, as it provides enough time to get to know each other while also allowing for individual reflection on feelings.
