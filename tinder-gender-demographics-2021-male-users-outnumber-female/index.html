Tinder Gender Demographics 2021: Male Users Outnumber Female


Tinder is one of the most popular dating apps in the world, with millions of users across the globe. However, it is also one of the most gender-imbalanced apps, with 75% of users identifying as male. In some countries, such as India, the male to female disparity is even larger, while in Europe it is closer to 50/50. Here is a look at the gender demographics of Tinder users in 2021.

Age Breakdown

The majority of Tinder users are aged 18-29, making up 65% of the user base. The second largest age group is 30-44, making up 24% of users. The remaining 11% of users are aged 45 and above.

Gender Breakdown

The gender breakdown of Tinder users in 2021 is heavily skewed towards males. 75% of users identify as male, while only 24% identify as female. This is a significant gender imbalance, with males outnumbering females by more than three to one.

Regional Breakdown

The gender imbalance of Tinder users varies by region. In India, the male to female ratio is even more skewed, with males making up 85% of users. In Europe, the gender ratio is closer to 50/50, with males making up 52% of users.
