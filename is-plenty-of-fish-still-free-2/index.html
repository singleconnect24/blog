Is Plenty of Fish still free?


Plenty of Fish (POF) is one of the most popular free online dating sites available today. It has been around since 2003 and has millions of active users. The question is, is Plenty of Fish still free? The answer is yes, Plenty of Fish is still free to use.

Creating a Profile
Creating a POF profile is completely free and can be done in a few simple steps. All you need to do is enter your basic information such as age, gender, location, and interests. Once you have completed this, you can start using the site to find potential matches.

Search Filters
POF offers a range of search filters that can be used to narrow down your search results. You can filter by age, location, interests, and more. This makes it easy to find people who share your interests and are located near you.

Messaging
Once you have found someone you are interested in, you can start messaging them. Messaging is completely free and you can send as many messages as you like. You can also receive messages from other users.

POF Live
POF Live is a feature that allows you to video chat with other users. This is a great way to get to know someone better before meeting up in person. POF Live is completely free and can be used as often as you like.

In conclusion, Plenty of Fish is still free to use. Creating a profile, using search filters, messaging, and POF Live are all free. This makes it a great option for those who are looking for a free online dating site.
